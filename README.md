# Superstore-Performance-Overview-2025-
Superstore Performance Overview 2025 is an interactive Tableau dashboard analyzing sales, profit, orders, and regional performance. It visualizes key business metrics across segments, categories, and states to identify top-performing and underperforming areas. 

# Superstore Performance Overview 2025 – Tableau Dashboard

## 1. Project Overview
This project presents an interactive Tableau dashboard built on the Superstore dataset to analyze the sales, profit, and order performance for the year 2025. The dashboard helps business teams understand regional performance, product behavior, and customer segment trends through clear visual insights.

---

## 2. Dashboard Features

### 2.1 KPI Summary
- **Total Sales:** £745.6K  | **+21.4% YoY**
- **Total Profit:** £95.9K  | **+16.0% YoY**
- **Total Orders:** 1.7K  | **+28.6% YoY**
- **Profit Ratio:** 12.9% | **–4.4% YoY decline**

These KPIs provide a quick snapshot of the business's overall yearly performance.

---

## 3. Sales Insights

### 3.1 Sales by Region
- **West:** £258.2K (37%)
- **East:** £216.6K (19%)
- **Central:** £147.9K (–1% decline)
- **South:** £122.9K (31%)

**Insight:** Central region requires improvement due to declining performance.

---

### 3.2 Sales by Segment
- **Consumer:** £334.9K (45%)
- **Corporate:** £248.2K (33%)
- **Home Office:** £162.4K (22%)

**Insight:** Consumer segment contributes nearly half of all revenue.

---

### 3.3 Sales by Category
- **Technology:** £272.4K (37%)
- **Office Supplies:** £253.8K (34%)
- **Furniture:** £219.3K (29%)

**Insight:** Technology leads overall sales, with Office Supplies showing consistent volume.

---

## 4. Geographic Analysis

### 4.1 Sales by State (Map View)
The dashboard maps sales across all U.S. states, highlighting strong clusters in:
- **California**
- **New York**
- **Texas**
- **East Coast region**

There are **10 states** with limited or unknown data.

**Insight:** Coastal states dominate revenue; central states lag behind.

---

## 5. Product Insights

### 5.1 Sales by Sub-Category
Top-performing sub-categories:
- **Phones:** £105.7K  
- **Chairs:** £73.7K  
- **Binders:** £62.9K  
- **Copiers:** £59.9K  
- **Tables:** £43.1K  

Lowest-performing sub-categories:
- **Fasteners:** £9.0K  
- **Labels:** £4.0K  

**Insight:** Phones and Chairs dominate sales; Labels and Fasteners show minimal demand.

## 7. Key Takeaways
1. West region is the top revenue driver.  
2. Technology category leads overall sales contribution.  
3. Phones and Chairs dominate sub-category revenue.  
4. YoY profit ratio dropped despite increasing sales—indicating higher costs/discounting.  
5. Underperforming regions and sub-categories require strategic focus.
